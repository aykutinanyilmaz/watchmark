Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Thank you for using WatchMark. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the WatchMark mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Authentication information

User Data

You may choose to store information including:

  • Watchlists
  • Watched history
  • Ratings
  • Favorites
  • App preferences

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Anonymous analytics

2. How We Use Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Synchronize your data across devices
  • Provide personalized features
  • Improve application performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Respond to support requests
  • Manage subscriptions
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. Third-Party Services

Firebase

Firebase is used for authentication, cloud storage, analytics, and crash reporting.

RevenueCat

RevenueCat manages subscription status and communicates with Apple's In-App Purchase system. Payment information is processed exclusively by Apple and is never stored by WatchMark.

TMDb

WatchMark uses The Movie Database (TMDb) API to provide movie and television information.

This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.

4. Subscriptions

Subscriptions are purchased through Apple's App Store. Billing, payments, renewals, and refunds are handled by Apple.

5. Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your account at any time using the Account Deletion feature available within the application. After deletion, your personal information and associated data will be permanently removed unless retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect your information. However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

WatchMark is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If personal information from a child is discovered, it will be deleted as soon as reasonably possible.

8. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request a copy of your data

9. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The updated version will always be published on this page with a revised Effective Date.
